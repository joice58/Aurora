The Design System · Editorial Steel

Work or life.
Work and life.

One idea runs through every frame: a home that refuses the trade-off. The art direction is Editorial Steel, an architecture-monograph feel, navy canvas, a single brushed-steel accent, oversized light type and massive negative space. The signature interaction makes the idea physical: Work or life resolves into Work and life, the platform for Live the AND Life.

01 · The Name

AURORA

the dawn · the northern light

Aurora, first light in a northern sky.

The aurora is energy made visible: ribbons of steel-blue light that cross the northern sky and join it end to end. That is the address in one word. North Bengaluru, rising, and light that connects rather than divides, the and, not the or. The name resolves the same way the work does: two things you were told to choose between, joined by a line of light.

02 · The Signature

The Trade-off Resolve

AND

A rooted home or a rising address

Calm or convenience

Nature at the door or the airport minutes away

The concept made physical. A trade-off line arrives as A or B, with B muted and struck through. On scroll into view, the strike retracts, B brightens, and or turns into and. Behind it drifts an oversized watermark, AND. The device appears everywhere: the hero, section turns, image reveals.

04 · Typography

One grotesque, light

The brand is locked to a single Helvetica-class grotesque, Inter. No serif, no second family. Range comes from weight and scale, not typeface: oversized light display, tight tracking, and the full stop used as a beat.

05 · Light & Photography

Two truths in one frame

The rules

  • Two truths in one frame, a calm, lived-in human moment and the rising corridor beyond.
  • Considered light, navy shadow, a soft cool glow.
  • People and the human moment before buildings.
  • Restraint & negative space, the empty margins carry as much weight as the subject.

Never

  • ✕Loud, flat daylight
  • ✕Flat fields of metal (steel is light, not plating)
  • ✕Gold, warm bling, or serif type
  • ✕Generic stock smiles
  • ✕Empty trophy interiors
  • ✕Fantasy skylines that deny the real, still-rising corridor
06 · In Practice

Motion, layout & voice

Motion

Scroll-triggered reveals that lift content into place, the or-to-and strike-retract resolve, clip-path image wipes, staggered entrances. Easing is [0.16, 1, 0.3, 1], and every motion respects prefers-reduced-motion.

Layout

Massive negative space. A single steel hairline as the rule. Numbered sections (01, 02, 03). An asymmetric editorial grid, an architecture-monograph feel.

Voice

A single grotesque, Inter, in light weights. Quietly confident, plain, exact. The full stop is the beat: Work near tomorrow. Live with everything else. Always endorsed, an Aratt Alchemy address.
