07 · The Gap

How they sell vs. where we win

What they all do

  • Everyone leads with the airport (“X minutes to KIA”), table-stakes, not a differentiator.
  • Brand-as-trust + investment/appreciation carry most of the argument.
  • Spec-and-amenity checklists in a rational tone; most have no emotional idea at all.
  • Price anchored at the entry (₹36-89 L), the corridor skews “affordable luxury”.

Where Aurora wins

  • Emotion vs. spec sheets, an experience platform almost nobody here has.
  • Concierge / service layer is completely unclaimed in this micro-market.
  • “Quiet confidence” cuts through a loud, feature-stacked street.
  • 2 & 3 BHK at 1,232 to 1,980 sq.ft., sell the life inside the home.

Watch-outs

  • MJR already uses “elevate your lifestyle” → lean on the unique refuse-the-trade-off idea (Live the AND Life), not generic “elevated”.
  • Provident already owns “wellness / sanctuary” → don't fight there.
  • Provident & Purva match on size → differentiate on brand & experience, not square feet.

The Aratt perception

A managed online image over a shaky organic record.

The planted layer

Page-one search is dominated by self-published “reviews”, Quora Spaces, Google Sites, WordPress, Issuu, uniformly glowing, clearly seeded.

The organic layer

MouthShut 1.65/5; a “Please avoid Aratt” resident review; forums cite delayed possession (some 7+ yrs), leakage/water ingress, weak fittings, poor post-sale CRM, cost gaps.

The fair positives

Genuine praise too, green/open layouts, amenities, security, decent materials, value. Quality is inconsistent, not absent.

⚠ The risk, Our buyers are research-driven engineers & IT professionals, they will find the 1.65/5 and sense the astroturf. “Trust the Aratt legacy,” asserted, backfires. Trust must be earned with proof.

What it means for the campaign

→

Trust is the campaign's hardest job, lead with proof (RERA, written possession commitment, delivered-site visits, third-party validation), not brand assertion.

→

Make WATER a visible, named promise (own source + treated + recycled + STP). The “everyday works better” claim dies if water doesn't.

→

Be honest about commute, sell airport & aerospace-job proximity (true), not city access (it isn't). Target north-working personas.

→

Turn infra-immaturity into the early-mover thesis, backed by the incoming metro / STRR / Airport City / schools timeline.

→

Retire astroturf for genuine advocacy, seed real resident proof and over-deliver on transparency to out-weigh the criticism.

10 · The Route

Communication route, v2

The organising idea

Refuse the trade-off. / Live the AND Life

Aurora, An Aratt Alchemy Address

Every home on this corridor asks the buyer to give something up: the location or the space, the address or the calm, the investment or a place worth living in. Aurora refuses that trade-off and holds both sides of the line. One platform carries the idea, “Live the AND Life”, resolved through the signature OR-to-AND interaction, work OR life becoming work AND life, all grounded in the same product: spacious 2 & 3 BHK homes, a concierge layer, minutes from the jobs on a green, low-density address.

Tone, Confident, not loud, but unmissable. Crafted restraint, quiet certainty over volume.

Why it holds, Anchors the emotion to the product itself: the location and the space, the address and the calm, the investment and a life worth living, each half provable, so the promise holds.

What we hardened · red-team → fix

✕

Present-vs-future tense clash

→ “Live the AND Life” unifies comfort-now with corridor-upside, both halves, not a bet on one.

✕

Lifestyle not deliverable on the ground yet

→ Sell foresight & trajectory, backed by corridor data, not a false present.

✕

Concierge unprovable & copyable

→ Charter-backed, named-operator, funded guarantee = real proof.

✕

Unknown name goes quiet = invisible

→ “An Aratt Alchemy address” + full-weight, high-SOV launch.

✕

Investor & value personas under-served

→ Dedicated NRI/investor numbers track + honest 2BHK value path.

✕

Analytical buyers bounce off the art

→ Dual-track funnel, emotion to attract, hard proof to convert.

✕

RERA / compliance exposure

→ Phase 0 gate: register first; RERA # + disclaimers on every asset.
